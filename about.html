About Holly

Holly has a compassion for the world. Her passion is lifting others up! She has championed the wellbeing of thousands of people through her 28 years as a R.N. and case manager. Through her writings she continues to champion her readers by cheering on, uplifting, and motivating them to do and be their best. Her message is simple, do what you can to help. She believes help at times is as simple as a smile or a hug. Her belief in Jesus Christ is where her compassion for the world was birthed. She is very proud to be a follower of Jesus Christ. Her belief in him colors her world.
Holly lives in Ohio with her husband Todd, daughters Olivia &Natalie and their Goldendoodle Bo. Holly and her husband do a great deal of philanthropy, again believing we should do what we can to help.
