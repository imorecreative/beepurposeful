BU: Identify Your Purpose and Live It

The guide to BEING YOU! Overcome fear and obstacles and feel empowered to live out your purpose and desiny!

BU is All About U!

Have you EVER wondered what you were born to do? Where is your life headed? What your purpose is? Well, this book helps you find those answers. The words purpose and destiny can be huge words that intimidate. But BU brings them down to a size you can work with. Bu makes it easy to identify your talents ,passions and purpose. Sometimes a little encouragement is all you need to make the next step. Encouragement fills every page . BU cheers you on like a supportive friend who has your back!!
